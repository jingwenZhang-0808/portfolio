Chatcat

Introduction: Chatcat is a sound interactive installation that developed based on Natural Language Processing (NLP). It represents my understanding about Human - Chatbot Relationship, intimacy and loneliness. Chatcat can offer intelligent responses to help fight loneliness. But its overall image is a cat. So it would not cause the problem of uncanny valley and virtual lovers. And it has soft hair and curing meows to make people feel warm and relaxed.

Chatcat 是一个自然语言处理 (NLP) 开发的声音交互装置。 它代表了我对人与聊天机器人关系、亲密和孤独的理解。 Chatcat可以提供智能的回复来帮助对抗孤独。 但它的整体形象是一只猫。所以不会造成恐怖谷和虚拟恋人的问题。 它有柔软的头发和治愈的喵喵声，让人感到温暖和放松。

Time: 2020.10- 2019.12

Type: Product Design

Tool: Cat Toy, Speech Recognition Module, Python

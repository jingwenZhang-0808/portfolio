In this work, I made an animated video as a 3D logo design for my name. In C4D, I added a glass material to the logo itself, and also set multi-color particles that are constantly generated behind it to create this gleaming feeling. Then I edited its animation in AE.

在这项作品中，我制作了一个动画视频作为我名字的 3D 标志设计。 在C4D中，我为logo本身加入了玻璃材质，还在其背后设置了不断产生的多色粒子，营造出这种闪闪发光的感觉。 然后我在AE中编辑了它的动画。

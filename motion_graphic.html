Joan Zhang

In this work, I made an animated video as a 3D logo design for my name. In C4D, I added a glass material to the logo itself, and also set multi-color particles that are constantly generated behind it to create this gleaming feeling. Then I edited its animation in AE.

在这项作品中，我制作了一个动画视频作为我名字的 3D 标志设计。 在C4D中，我为logo本身加入了玻璃材质，还在其背后设置了不断产生的多色粒子，营造出这种闪闪发光的感觉。 然后我在AE中编辑了它的动画。

Info

Time: 2021

Type: Logo Animation

Tool: C4D, AE, AI

Aquarium

One of Saint-Saëns` work, The Carnival of the Animals - VII. Aquarium, is my favorite music about depicting marine life. So out of my own interest, I choose to use animation to realize this melody. These light jellyfish are the protagonists in this animation because I want to show the agility of water creature.

圣桑的作品之一，动物狂欢节 - VII。 水族馆，是我最喜欢的描绘海洋生物的音乐。 所以出于自己的兴趣，我尝试用动画呈现这段旋律。 这些轻盈的水母是动画的主角，因为我想展示水生物所特有的灵动。

Info

Time: 2021

Type: Visual Music

Tool: AE, AI

Clannad

This is my attempt to animate with text only. The text and music come from Clannad's ending song, Dango Daikazoku. The characters of Japanese hiragana are all round and cute, and the original episode represents the wish of a cute girl. So in this work, I want each character to be in a lively state, like acting as falling raindrop, little girl shaking her head and winking stars.

这是我一次仅使用文字制作动画的尝试。文字和音乐来自 Clannad 的片尾曲，团子大家族。因为日文平假名的字符都圆润可爱，原著代表了一位少女的心愿。所以在这部作品中，我希望每个字符都表现的活泼轻快，就像坠落的雨滴，摇头晃脑的小女孩和眨眼睛的星星。

Info

Time: 2021

Type: Kinetic Typography

Tool: AE, AI

Halloween

This is the title sequence I remade for the horror movie Halloween. I extracted three core images, ghosts, window and eyes, from the original plot. And I regrouped them in different ways to create a kind of thrilling atmosphere.

这是我为恐怖电影万圣节重新制作的片头。 我从原始情节中提取了三个核心图像元素，鬼魂、窗户和眼睛。 我将它们以不同的方式重新组合，以营造出一种惊悚恐怖的氛围。
